Love Hunter
===========================
This is a tiny treasure-hunter "game" I created in 25/08/2011, to celebrate my 4 years love anniversary with the beautiful lady Ngoc Hien. Play it here: http://ngochien.huydung.com/anniversary/index.php

How to play (for Ngoc Hien)
===========================
* Ngoc Hien have to guess where are the 15 special places that we have sweet memory together in Hanoi, with no hints
* She drags the heart marker around the google map, and in-case the heart is near to one special place, the place will be unlocked
* If she can complete all 15 places, she'll be redirected to a special video I made just for her!

Well, and she DID complete the challenge, in around 20 minutes. What an achievement!

For guests
============================
The code have a variable $is_hien, and when you access the game online here: http://ngochien.huydung.com/anniversary/index.php, it'll be set to false. In that case, you only have 8 places to find, with clear hint for each. And you can even go directly to the movie, by following the link in the footer :)
